# Client Notes

## Project Background

`eventtrashbox.com` is a proposed new website focused on selling or promoting a
small set of event-related cardboard box products.

The idea came from Client, owner of CompanyBox, an established business that
prints custom cardboard boxes and produces boxes for major clients such as
FedEx. CompanyBox is important origin and backend context, but it should not be
referenced in the customer-facing EventTrashBox experience unless the client
explicitly changes that direction.

Initial product focus:

- Event Trash Boxes
- Booth Trash Boxes

Early direction from Client:

- Build a simple website.
- Focus on the two existing products first.
- Marketing and social media are already being considered by someone else.
- Full branding and content direction are not yet defined.

## Positioning

**Question:** Is eventboxes.com meant to be a simple focused sales page for the two existing trash box products, or should it feel like a separate business from CompanyBox?

**Answer:** Separate business entirely, with the exception of ties to backend services including, but not limited to, Shopify Plus, HubSpot, CloudFlow, etc.

**Question:** Should customers know Event Boxes is powered by CompanyBox, or should CompanyBox entirely disappear from the experience?

**Answer:** No reference to CompanyBox.

## Ordering And Customization

**Question:** When someone clicks Order or Customize, should they stay on Event Boxes, go into the normal CompanyBox ordering/design flow, or submit a quote/artwork request?

**Answer:** Stay on Event Boxes and launch “Event Boxes Builder.”

**Question:** When customizing, should it use the same builder, or should it have the same basic functionality but look different?

**Answer:** Yes — same builder, but different look.

## Site Scope

**Question:** For launch, do you want one focused landing page or multiple pages, such as product pages, FAQ, contact/quote request, and gallery?

**Answer:** Multiple pages: Landing, FAQ, product page, Contact, quote request, and gallery.

## Brand And Assets

**Question:** Do you already have a logo, colors, tagline, written content, product photos, renderings, or marketing materials?

**Answer:** Nothing yet.

## Ecommerce And CRM

**Question:** Should it use Shopify? If not, what else? If so, it will say CompanyBox when checking out.

**Answer:** Yes. My understanding is that with our current Shopify Plus plan, we can have up to 10 separate stores. We can plan to use one of these seats for this company. We will also need to make sure this site is tied to our HubSpot CRM.
